The bi-fold accordion door

Experience a multi-panel door.

These aren't traditional doors. The Panoramic Doors system replaces a wall with up to four lightweight panels, turning a closed room into open air in a single motion.

Each panel moves independently along a single bottom-weighted track — slide one, pivot the rest, or stack the entire wall to one side. The patented Slide-Pivot-Stack design eliminates the bulky folding hardware found in traditional bi-fold doors.

Panels per door, one single track
2–4
  1. 01

    Hingeless, single-track design.

    Individual panels move independently along a bottom-weighted track — no hinges, no folding hardware, no major structural changes to your home.

  2. 02

    Indoors becomes outdoors.

    Open the full wall in one motion. Panels stack neatly to one side, leaving an unobstructed view between living space and lanai.

  3. 03

    Configured to your home.

    Open left, right, or as a butterfly split. Available in a wide range of colors and finishes to match the house.
